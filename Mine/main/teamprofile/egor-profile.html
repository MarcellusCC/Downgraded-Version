Egor (墨瑟福)

Writer / Designer

Hello everyone, my name is Egor Losev, and I am currently studying at BIT and majoring in Computer Science and Technology. Our website project is built on a simple yet meaningful idea: implementing an ELO system with the objective of helping students learn and improve their skills through friendly competition. As there would be a leaderboard with the best learners. The goal is to make studying more fun and also to provide students with a structured way to track their learning progress.

My role in the team focuses on writing and designing the website. This means I am responsible for choosing the right colors, coming up with text, and shaping the overall layout so that the website looks professional, clean, and easy to navigate, regardless of the device used. I truly believe that good design is just as important as the inside content, because a pleasingly designed interface motivates its users to keep using the platform.

Through this project, I hope be able to combine both creativity and programming skills. I will try my best to balance aesthetic choices with usability and simplicity, as making sure the coders won't have a hard time implementing all the things into one. Moreover, the website should remain robust while still being visually appealing. Overall, this experience will truly be valuable to me and my peers, as it shall allow me to apply what I have learned to a real project and thus work effectively as part of a team in the future.
