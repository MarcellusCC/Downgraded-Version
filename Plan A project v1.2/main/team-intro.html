Our Mission

We are Team 淘宝 (Team Taobao), a dedicated group of computer science students committed to revolutionizing AI education through innovative web technologies. Our e-learning platform combines gamification with comprehensive AI curriculum to create an engaging learning experience that adapts to each user's skill level.

Just like Taobao connects buyers and sellers in a vast marketplace, our team connects learners with knowledge through our innovative AI learning platform. We create a marketplace of ideas where students can discover, explore, and master artificial intelligence concepts. Our target audience includes students, professionals, and anyone curious about AI - whether you're a complete beginner or looking to advance your existing knowledge.
